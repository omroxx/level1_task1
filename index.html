    THIRTY NINE YEARS OF

    T-SERIES

Welcome to the official Thirty Nine Years Of T-Series Music Company.
T-Series was founded on 11 July 1983 by Gulshan Kumar,
at the time a fruit juice seller in the Daryaganj neighbourhood of Delhi.
The company initially sold pirated Bollywood songs,
prior to releasing original music.Back then,
the Indian audio cassette market was small-scale, with widespread piracy,
but there was growing demand for cassette music. According to Rediff,
while Kumar "was involved in piracy,
he was passionately market- and consumer-driven." The company also took
advantage of loopholes in copyright law allowing for the release of cover versions of songs,
which T-Series would then flood the market with
